1. Abstraction is one of the main features of database systems. 

2. Hiding irrelevant details from user and providing abstract view of data to users, 
   helps in easy and efficient user-database interaction

3. in three level of DBMS architecture, The top level of that architecture is “view level”. 
   The view level provides the “view of data” to the users and hides the irrelevant details 
   such as data relationship, database schema, constraints, security etc from the user.
