1. steps in ER-diagramme Design.
    1. identify entities in DB.
    2. identify attributes of each entities and their type (derived , singl valued or multi-values ,etc.)
    3. identify relationships and constraints between entities.

2. before making any DB we have to note down all the requirements of the DB user.
   either in text form or by interviewing them or assuming some requirements.
